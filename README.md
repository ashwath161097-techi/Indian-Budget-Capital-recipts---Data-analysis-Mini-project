# Indian-Budget-Capital-recipts---Data-analysis-Mini-project
This project analyzes the fiscal performance of capital receipts over the financial years 2017–2025 using data visualization and business intelligence techniques. The study focuses on evaluating revenue trends, growth patterns, and the comparative performance of Budget, Revised, and Actual estimates.

# Project Objectives
1.	Analyses Year-wise Financial Trends
To examine the growth pattern of capital receipts from 2017 to 2024 and identify expansion and decline phases.
2.	Evaluate Budget vs Actual Performance
To assess the gap between projected (Budget), adjusted (Revised), and realized (Actual) capital receipts.
3.	Measure Year-over-Year Growth
To calculate and interpret YoY growth percentages in order to evaluate financial momentum and stability.
4.	Study Revenue Structure Composition
To analyze the proportional contribution of different estimate types and identify dependency patterns.
5.	Develop an Interactive Dashboard
To create a professional Power BI dashboard with KPIs, trend analysis, category comparison, and growth insights.
6.	Provide Strategic Recommendations
To suggest improvements in forecasting accuracy, revenue realization, and long-term fiscal sustainability.

# Problem Statement
Capital receipts play a crucial role in determining fiscal strength and long-term financial sustainability. However, fluctuations in revenue, differences between budgeted and actual figures, and variations in year-over-year growth create challenges in evaluating true financial performance.
Between 2017 and 2024, capital receipts have experienced periods of growth and decline. There is a noticeable gap between Budget, Revised, and Actual estimates, making it difficult to assess forecasting accuracy and execution efficiency. Additionally, changes in revenue composition over time raise concerns regarding dependency on projected estimates rather than realized revenue.
Without a structured analytical approach, it becomes challenging to:
•	Identify long-term growth trends
•	Measure performance stability
•	Evaluate gaps between projected and actual receipts
•	Detect peak and decline phases
•	Support strategic fiscal decision-making
Therefore, this project aims to analyze the fiscal performance of capital receipts using data visualization and business intelligence tools to uncover trends, measure growth, and provide actionable insights for improving forecasting accuracy and financial sustainability.
